IT Specialist

Join our IT team and help us develop the technological infrastructure that supports our impactful work in the field.

Role Overview

The IT Specialist is responsible for managing and maintaining the technological systems that support our organization’s operations. You will ensure the security of our IT infrastructure, troubleshoot issues, and ensure all systems run efficiently.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage and optimize the organization’s IT infrastructure.
  • Ensure cybersecurity protocols are followed to protect sensitive data.
  • Provide technical support for staff and volunteers in the field.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve hardware and software issues.
  • Assist with the development and implementation of new technological solutions.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • At least 2 years of experience in IT systems management or related roles.
  • Strong knowledge of IT infrastructure, networking, and security protocols.
  • Problem-solving skills and the ability to work under pressure.
  • Good communication skills to interact with various stakeholders.
